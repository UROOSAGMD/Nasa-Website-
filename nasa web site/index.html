NASA’s SpaceX CRS-32 Docking at Space Station

A SpaceX Dragon spacecraft, carrying approximately 6,700 pounds of cargo to the International Space Station for NASA, lifted off at 4:15 a.m. EDT Monday, April 21, from Launch Complex 39A at Kennedy Space Center in Florida. Live coverage of the spacecraft’s arrival at the orbiting laboratory will begin at 6:45 a.m. EDT on Tuesday, April 22.
